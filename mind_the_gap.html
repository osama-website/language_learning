    How to Play

    • Choose the correct word to fill in the blank for each sentence.
    • Click the button next to any option to see real-world usage examples.
    • Click the Category tag on a question, or use the Rules icon at the top, to study grammar rules.
    • Use the Filters icon to focus your practice on specific grammar categories or problem types.
    • Track your accuracy, progress, and streaks using the Stats icon.
    • Switch between Beginner, Intermediate, and Expert difficulties in the Settings menu.
